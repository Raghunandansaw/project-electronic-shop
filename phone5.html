    Oppo Reno 13 mobile was launched on 25th November 2024. The phone comes with a 120 Hz refresh rate 6.83-inch touchscreen display offering a resolution of 2800x1272 pixels (FHD+) at a pixel density of 450 pixels per inch (ppi). Oppo Reno 13 Pro is powered by a 3.35 MHz octa-core processor. It comes with 16GB of RAM. The Oppo Reno 13 Pro runs Android 15 and is powered by a 5,800mAh non-removable battery. The Oppo Reno 13 Pro supports wireless charging, as well as 80W Fast Charging fast charging. As far as the cameras are concerned, the Oppo Reno 13 Pro on the rear packs a triple camera setup featuring a 50-megapixel (f/1.8) primary camera; an 8-megapixel (f/2.2, ultra wide-angle) camera, and a 50-megapixel (f/2.8, telephoto) camera. It has a single front camera setup for selfies, featuring a 50-megapixel sensor with an f/2.0 aperture. The Oppo Reno 13 Pro runs ColorOS 15 is based on Android 15 and packs 1TB of inbuilt storage. The Oppo Reno 13 Pro is a dual-SIM (GSM + CDMA and GSM + CDMA) mobile that accepts Nano-SIM and Nano-SIM cards. The Oppo Reno 13 Pro measures 162.73 x 76.55 x 7.55mm (height x width x thickness) and weighs 197.00 grams. It was launched in Midnight Black, Starlight Pink, and Butterfly Purple colours. It features an IP69 rating for dust and water protection. Connectivity options on the Oppo Reno 13 Pro include Wi-Fi 802.11 ax, GPS, Bluetooth v5.40, NFC, USB OTG, USB Type-C, 4G , and 5G. Sensors on the phone include accelerometer, ambient light sensor, compass/ magnetometer, gyroscope, proximity sensor, and in-display fingerprint sensor. The Oppo Reno 13 Pro supports face unlock.
