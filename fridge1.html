  • Whirlpool Refridgerator

    Rs. 27,999
    BUY

    This Whirlpool 192 L refrigerator is a single-door model designed for small families of 3-4 members. It features Insulated Capillary Technology, which enhances cooling efficiency and ensures up to 9 hours of cooling retention during power outages. This technology also contributes to better compressor performance and faster cooling. The refrigerator operates without the need for an external stabilizer, handling voltage fluctuations between 130V and 300V. With a total capacity of 192 liters, it includes ample space for fresh food storage and a separate freezer compartment. The interior is equipped with four drawers, two toughened glass shelves, and an antibacterial gasket. Special features include easy manual defrosting, jumbo storage for up to three 2-liter bottles, an auto-connect function for home inverters, a large vegetable crisper, Honey Comb Lock-in technology, and a Quick Chill Zone.
